The Pilot Program

What this partnership
means for Bowen

Three ways the Bowen–Embra partnership creates lasting value for students and faculty.

01

Bowen shapes the platform

As the founding partner, Bowen students and faculty provide direct feedback that determines how Embra works — before any other institution has access. The tools built here will reflect Bowen’s values and standards.

02

Real outcomes, tracked

Embra measures what matters — student engagement, mentor match quality, and preparation confidence. All data is shared directly and transparently with Bowen administration throughout the semester.

03

A legacy of leadership

The success of this pilot positions Bowen as a leader in legal education innovation. When other law schools adopt Embra, they will be following the precedent Bowen set as the first.

From the Founder

“Bowen is not just our first school — Bowen is the proof point that legal education and technology can grow together with integrity. Every student who joins this pilot is helping build something that will serve thousands of law students long after them.”

— Adrian Bond  ·  Founder & CEO, Embra

Student Data Protection

Built FERPA-compliant from the ground up. No student data is shared, sold, or used beyond the scope of the educational relationship.

Faculty & Administration Access

Bowen leadership receives full visibility into platform usage, engagement metrics, and program health through a dedicated partner dashboard.

Crisis Response Protocol

A documented escalation framework is in place for any student wellness disclosures, in coordination with Bowen’s existing support infrastructure.

End-of-Semester Report

A comprehensive impact report is delivered to Bowen administration at the close of each semester — fully documenting outcomes, engagement, and recommendations.
